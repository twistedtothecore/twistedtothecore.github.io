A brief Bit About Myself

Ive had a life long interest in Technology
From my earliest time typing away at a dos command line
to building websites and basic apps in highschool,
To diving into Digital Photography and photo editing.
I took a detour from tech in my mid 20's and got into logistics
and transportation, gaining valueable insights into culture,
and business from coast to coast, dealing with tough schedules,
difficult projects with tight deadlines and overcoming unforseen
complications to always meet those deadlines.
Now I find myself rediscovering Tech and remembering how much
I enjoy creating.

My photography is featured thru out this site.
